## What I Do

I'm a software developer with 4+ years of experience helping businesses ship tools that save their teams time. I partner directly with companies — not as a marketplace gig, but as the engineer who takes a problem from "this is slow and manual" to "this runs itself."

My specialty is **Office Add-in development** — custom tools built into Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint that fit the way a business already works. Around that, I build **web applications, admin portals, and AI integrations** that connect the rest of the workflow.

If your team is copying data between systems, formatting documents by hand, or waiting on a process that should be automatic — that's the kind of problem I solve.
